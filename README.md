# Hitori-Days (ひとりデイズ) #
**"A cozy walking simulator RPG fan game of the beloved Japanese animation series Bocchi The Rock!"**

Dive into a 16x16 pixel-art world as Hitori "Bocchi" Gotoh. This is a low-stress, atmospheric RPG designed to capture the quiet (and anxious) moments of a guitar hero's daily life.

**Current Features** - (Might be changed)
Radio System: Listen to curated tracks (including local high-fidelity backups) while you explore.
16x16 ROM Aesthetic: Pure retro visuals powered by GameMaker.
Efficient Build: A lightweight experience

**The Roadmap (Soon™)**
Quest System: Help the members of Kessoku Band and the local community. (Avoid Ryo, she'll blow your cash)
Open World Exploration: Navigate through Shimokitazawa and beyond.
Money & Economy: Work part-time jobs at STARRY to fund your gear.
Gig System: Play songs with band (yaaaay)
Pseudo DAW: A conceptual music creation tool within the game. (this might get scrapped though www)

Built With
Engine: GameMaker.
Libraries: Scribble (Text Renderer, by JujuAdams).
Logic: Custom C++ and GML.
